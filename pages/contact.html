Frequently Asked Questions

How can I get support for game issues?

For any technical issues with our games, please email our support team at support@digitaldreams.com with details about the issue, your device, and operating system. Our team aims to respond within 24 hours on business days.

Do you offer refunds for purchases?

Yes, we offer refunds for eligible purchases within 14 days of purchase. Please refer to our Refund Policy for complete details on eligibility and the refund process.

How can I report a bug in a game?

You can report bugs by emailing support@digitaldreams.com with the subject line "Bug Report: [Game Name]". Include details about the bug, steps to reproduce it, and your device information to help us investigate.

Are you hiring game developers?

We're always looking for talented individuals to join our team! Check out our careers page for current openings, or send your resume and portfolio to careers@digitaldreams.com for future opportunities.

Can I integrate your games into my platform?

For business partnership inquiries or integration requests, please contact our business development team at partnerships@digitaldreams.com with details about your platform and integration needs.

Where can I find press materials for your games?

Press kits for our games are available upon request for verified media outlets. Please contact our media relations team at press@digitaldreams.com to request access to our press materials.
