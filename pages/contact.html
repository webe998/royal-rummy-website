Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about DIGITAL DREAMS games and services.

How do I get technical support for a game issue?

For technical support, please email support@digitaldreams.com with details about your issue, including your device model, operating system version, and a description of the problem. Our support team typically responds within 24 hours on business days.

What is your refund policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all our premium games. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, contact our support team with your order details to process a refund. Please note that in-app purchases are subject to the refund policies of their respective app stores.

How do I report a bug in one of your games?

Bug reports are essential for improving our games. Please email support@digitaldreams.com with "Bug Report" in the subject line, and include detailed information about the bug, steps to reproduce it, and screenshots if possible. Our development team reviews all bug reports and prioritizes fixes based on severity.

Do you have job opportunities available?

Yes, we're always looking for talented individuals passionate about gaming. Visit our About Us page and click on the Careers section to see current openings. If you don't see a position that matches your skills but believe you'd be a great addition to our team, send your resume to careers@digitaldreams.com.

How can I integrate DIGITAL DREAMS games on my platform?

For business partnerships and integration opportunities, please contact our business development team at business@digitaldreams.com. We offer various integration options for websites, mobile apps, and gaming platforms, along with custom solutions for specific requirements.

Do you offer press materials for your games?

Yes, we provide press kits for all our game releases. These include high-resolution images, logos, gameplay videos, and press releases. For media inquiries or to request press materials, please contact our PR team at press@digitaldreams.com.
