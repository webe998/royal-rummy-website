Refund Policy

Understanding our game purchase and in-app refund procedures.

Last Updated: June 1, 2023

Note: This refund policy applies to purchases made directly through DIGITAL DREAMS. For purchases made through third-party platforms (such as Apple App Store, Google Play Store, Steam, etc.), please refer to the respective platform's refund policy.

1. Introduction

At DIGITAL DREAMS, we aim to provide high-quality games and a satisfying experience for all our players. We understand that sometimes purchases may not meet your expectations or you may experience technical issues. This Refund Policy outlines the circumstances under which we offer refunds for our games and in-app purchases.

By purchasing our games or making in-app purchases, you agree to the terms of this Refund Policy.

2. Refund Eligibility

Refunds may be available in the following circumstances:

  • Technical Issues: If our games or purchased content suffer from significant technical issues that substantially impair your ability to play or use them, and these issues cannot be resolved through customer support.
  • Unauthorized Purchases: If purchases were made without your authorization, such as by a minor or through fraudulent means.
  • Non-Delivery: If you paid for content that was not delivered to your account.
  • Significant Game Changes: If we make substantial changes to a game that significantly affect gameplay after your purchase.

Refund requests are evaluated on a case-by-case basis, taking into account the specific circumstances of each request.

3. Game Purchases

For full game purchases made directly through our website or services:

  • 14-Day Window: You may request a refund within 14 days of purchase if you have not downloaded or streamed the game, and we have not begun performance of the service.
  • Downloaded Games: If you have downloaded or played the game, you may still be eligible for a refund within 14 days if the game suffers from significant technical issues that substantially impair gameplay, and these issues cannot be resolved through our customer support.
  • Pre-ordered Games: You may cancel a pre-order and receive a full refund at any time before the release date. After release, our standard refund policy applies.

4. In-App Purchases

For in-app purchases, such as virtual currency, items, or game upgrades:

  • Virtual Currency and Items: Generally, all purchases of virtual currency, items, or other in-game benefits are final and non-refundable once the virtual currency or items have been added to your account.
  • Unused Virtual Currency: If virtual currency has been purchased but not spent or used in any way, we may consider a refund within 48 hours of purchase.
  • Accidental Purchases: We understand that accidental purchases can happen. For first-time accidental purchases, we may offer a one-time courtesy refund, provided the virtual items or currency have not been used.
  • Unauthorized Purchases: If you believe that unauthorized purchases have been made on your account (e.g., by a minor), please contact our customer support immediately.

Please note that refunds for in-app purchases are generally more restrictive due to the immediate delivery and consumable nature of virtual goods and currency.

5. Subscription Services

For any subscription-based services we offer:

  • Cancellation: You may cancel a subscription at any time. Your subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing period.
  • Free Trial Conversion: If you cancel during a free trial period, you will not be charged for the subscription.
  • Pro-rated Refunds: We generally do not provide pro-rated refunds for unused portions of a subscription period.
  • Automatic Renewal: Subscriptions automatically renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. If you forget to cancel and are charged for a renewal, contact customer support within 48 hours for assistance.

6. Refund Process

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

How to Request a Refund:

  1. Go to our Customer Support page.
  2. Select "Refund Request" as the inquiry type.
  3. Provide your order number or transaction ID.
  4. Explain the reason for your refund request.
  5. Include any relevant supporting information or evidence, such as screenshots of technical issues.

After submitting your request:

  • We will acknowledge receipt of your request within 48 hours.
  • Our team will review your request and may ask for additional information if needed.
  • We aim to provide a decision within 7 business days of receiving all necessary information.
  • If approved, refunds will typically be processed within 10 business days and will be issued through the original payment method when possible.

7. Exclusions and Limitations

The following circumstances generally do not qualify for a refund:

  • Dissatisfaction with game mechanics, difficulty, or creative elements
  • Changes in game balance or design that are part of normal game updates
  • Technical issues resulting from your hardware or software configuration that do not affect the majority of users
  • Purchases made more than 14 days prior to the refund request (except in the case of unauthorized purchases)
  • Virtual currency or items that have been spent or used in the game
  • Banned accounts due to violations of our Terms of Service
  • Purchases where you have violated the terms of a promotion or discount

8. Platform-Specific Policies

For purchases made through third-party platforms, the following policies apply:

8.1 Mobile Platforms

Purchases made through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store are subject to their respective refund policies:

  • Apple App Store: Please contact Apple Support or visit your purchase history in the App Store to request a refund.
  • Google Play Store: Visit the Google Play Store, go to "Account," then "Purchase history" to request a refund.

8.2 PC Platforms

Purchases made through PC gaming platforms are subject to their respective refund policies:

  • Steam: Steam offers refunds for games and DLC purchased through their platform within 14 days of purchase and with less than 2 hours of playtime.
  • Epic Games Store: The Epic Games Store offers refunds for games within 14 days of purchase and with less than 2 hours of playtime.

8.3 Console Platforms

Purchases made through console marketplaces are subject to their respective refund policies:

  • PlayStation Store: Contact PlayStation Support for their current refund policy.
  • Xbox Store: Visit the Microsoft account website to request a refund for digital games.
  • Nintendo eShop: Contact Nintendo Customer Support for information about their refund policy.

If you have any questions about our Refund Policy or need assistance with a refund request, please don't hesitate to contact us:
